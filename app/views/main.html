Our purpose is to make Internet-based services better for everyone.

Paradrop replaces today's Wi-Fi router with an intelligent platform capable of handling the fast-paced, complex, and evolving Internet of tomorrow.

Paradrop is an intelligent platform that lives on Wi-Fi routers, where special applications can be installed by the owner. These apps enable your router, the nerve center of all Internet in the home, to solve complex problems in ways that are impossible using today's routers.
