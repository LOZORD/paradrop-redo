Our purpose is to make Internet-based services better and easier for everyone.

Paradrop replaces today's Wi-Fi router with an intelligent platform capable of handling the fast-paced, complex, and evolving Internet of tomorrow.

Paradrop lives on Wi-Fi routers, where special applications can be installed by the owner. These apps enable your router, the nerve center of all Internet in the home, to solve complex problems in ways that are impossible using today's routers.

Applications installed on Paradrop routers benefit users by being simpler, more secure, and more private. We achieve this by keeping content that would normally go to the cloud inside of the home instead.

Paradrop was created by researchers out of the University of Wisconsin-Madison. For more information about us, please visit our about page.
